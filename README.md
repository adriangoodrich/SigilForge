# SigilForge
Forge your own arcane power with custom sigils

SigilForge is a magical circle and arcane sigil generator inspired by the fantasy worlds of anime and role-playing games.

Like a true craftsman of the mysterious, you'll be able to create, combine, and customize symbols of power—each line and curve imbued with magical intent. Whether you're illustrating your personal grimoire, designing your character's emblem, or simply exploring mystical art, SigilForge is your digital workshop where magic takes shape.
